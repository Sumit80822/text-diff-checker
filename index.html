‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How does the text diff checker work?

Our tool uses advanced algorithms to compare texts word by word, highlighting additions in green and deletions in red. It analyzes the longest common subsequence to provide accurate difference detection.

Is my text data secure?

Yes! All text comparison happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to external servers, ensuring complete privacy and security of your content.

Can I compare large text files?

Our tool handles reasonably large text files efficiently. For extremely large files (over 50,000 characters), performance may vary depending on your device.

What's the difference between ignoring case and whitespace?

Ignore case: Treats "Hello" and "hello" as identical. Ignore whitespace: Ignores extra spaces, tabs, and line breaks when comparing.
